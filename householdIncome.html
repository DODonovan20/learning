Income refers to all wealth received over a period of time.

    Major Sources of Income

  • Wages and Salaries: income from employment.

  • Pensions: paid to those who have retired from work.

  • Child Benefit: a payment made to parents of children.

  • Jobseeker's Benefit: a paymrnt to those who are unable to find employment.

  • Family Income Supplement: a top up payment to haouseholds with low levels of income.

  • Interest on Savings: a reward for saving money in banks, credit unions, etc.

  • Dividends on Shares: shareholders are owner of a company. They may be rewarded for investment by a portion of company profits called a dividend.

  • Rental Income: income received from rent on propeties.

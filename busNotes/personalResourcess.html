Personal Resources

A resource is anything that we can use to meet our needs or our goals. e.g. material, goods, knowledge and time.
Resources: help us achive things, are useful or valuable, and are often limited or scarce.

Types of Resources:

  1. Physical/Capital rsources: - Goods that are made by people and which can be used to provide other goods or services. E.g. buildings, property, computers , equipment, phones, books.

  2. Natural Rescources: - Households may have access to goods provided by nature. E.g. land water

  3. Financil Resources: - Includes all types of money available to households or individuals They allow people to access goods/services. E.g. employment income, child benefit, savings
